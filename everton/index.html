About

Hi! I'm Everton Bin

I graduated as a civil engineer, but I really fell in love when I first heard of Data Science. Being able to access data, extract precious insights and information from them and even create machine learning models to predict the future was like a dream to me.

So finally I decided to drop my career as a structural engineer and immersed myself deeply in learning and improving the skills, such as programming languages, business and statistics, to properly solve problems in this new big data world.

One of the most important lessons I've learnd from this journey was that learning is an ongoing process and that, as long as we are willing to learn, there is no limit to what we can achieve.
